What is meant by color balance?
Color balance can add or remove colors from photos as well as change the intensity of certain colors.

What are Hue and Saturation?
Hue changes the range of colors in an image whereas saturation can make colors more vibrant or muted.

Who owns this file after the considerable changes you have made to the images?
The original uploader or person who took/created the image/photo.

What is meant by ownership, pixels, binary representation
Ownership defines who has commercial rights over specific content.
Pixels are the the smallest element in a display or image. It is only 1 color and many pixel are used to create a single image.
Binary representation is the binary form of a file type. Binary representations consists solely of 1's and 0's
